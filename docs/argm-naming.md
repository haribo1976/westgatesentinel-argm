# ARGM: Naming Rationale

**Licence:** CC-BY-SA 4.0

---

## Selected Name: ARGM (Agentic Runtime Governance Model)

- **A**gentic: specifies technology class (agents, not AI in general)
- **R**untime: distinguishes from lifecycle governance and adoption readiness
- **G**overnance: positions in governance domain, not security or adoption
- **M**odel: aligns with maturity model convention without trademark proximity to CMMI

## Names Avoided

- **AISM:** used by Cyber Strategy Institute and AISM Institute
- **AIMS:** acronym for AI Management System (ISO 42001)
- **CMMI-AI or AI-CMM:** trademark proximity to ISACA
- **SAFE:** used by AI SAFE2 and SAFe (Scaled Agile)
- **TRUST:** overloaded (McKinsey AI Trust, EU Trustworthy AI)
- **GAIA:** used by multiple AI benchmarks

## Alternatives Considered

- **AROG** (Agentic Runtime Operations Governance): "a rogue" connotation
- **RAGA** (Runtime Agent Governance Assessment): "raga" is established music term
